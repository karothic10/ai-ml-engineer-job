# ai-ml-engineer-job
Hiring an ai / ml engineer

Company Description

Cortex Capital Intelligence is a boutique AI-driven advisory service for fintech lenders, helping finance teams make high-stakes lending and portfolio decisions with confidence.
We operate differently from traditional software companies. 
Rather than building generic products, we design bespoke AI decision systems for each client, tailored to their lending models, risk frameworks, and portfolio strategy.
Our work sits at the intersection of machine learning, financial risk analysis, and executive decision-making.
As a contractor, you will work directly with leadership and client finance teams to build practical, interpretable AI systems that inform real financial decisions.

Role Description

We are seeking a self-directed Applied Machine Learning Engineer to collaborate on building AI-powered decision infrastructure for fintech lenders.
This role focuses on developing predictive models and analytical systems using structured financial data to support lending, risk monitoring, and portfolio management decisions.
You will work with high autonomy to translate business problems into robust, interpretable machine learning solutions that can be used in real operational environments.

Example Problems You May Work On

Projects may include building systems to:
* Detect early warning signals of borrower covenant breaches
* Predict deterioration in loan portfolios
* Identify fraud or anomalous borrower behavior
* Build liquidity or runway risk early-warning models
* Monitor portfolio-level risk concentration and performance trends

Responsibilities:

* Develop predictive models and decision-support systems using structured financial datasets
* Design feature engineering pipelines and model evaluation frameworks
* Translate business problems into practical ML solutions
* Build interpretable and auditable models suitable for financial decision-making
* Collaborate with leadership and occasionally client teams to refine modeling objectives
* Deliver clean, documented, production-ready code
This is a high-ownership role with minimal bureaucracy and significant autonomy.

Requirements:
* 3+ years of experience in applied machine learning or data science
* Strong proficiency in Python and ML libraries such as scikit-learn, XGBoost, or LightGBM
* Experience building predictive models using structured data
* Strong skills in feature engineering, model validation, and performance evaluation
* Ability to translate business problems into analytical models
* Experience working independently and delivering production-quality ML work
* Familiarity with data pipelines, ETL processes, and cloud environments (AWS, GCP, or Azure)

Nice to Have:
* Experience in fintech, lending, banking, or capital markets
* Knowledge of risk modeling or portfolio analytics
* Experience building interpretable ML models for financial decision-making
* Experience building analytical dashboards or decision tools (Streamlit, Dash, Plotly)

Why Work With Us:
* High-impact projects — your work informs real financial decisions
* Direct exposure to fintech executives and decision-makers
* Lean and fast-moving environment. No corporate bureaucracy
* High autonomy in technical implementation
* Opportunity for repeat projects and long-term collaboration
Compensation & Engagement:

We work on fixed-price, project-based engagements and we are open to longer-term collaborations with high-performing contractors.


This is a remote position, worldwide.
